We present a restoration of the full scholarly apparatus of Dasent's original 1861 two-volume edition, including the translator's preface, introduction, and essays on Icelandic life in the Middle Ages—over 70,000 words of Dasent's work that has never before been available in ebook form.

The text was set in Sorts Mill Goudy and League Spartan, typefaces created by The League of Moveable Type.
